KDD 2022 Poster Preparation Instructions

Congratulations on your paper acceptance for KDD 2022. Please find below details for the poster presentation and schedule for your paper.

Who Should Prepare Posters? By default, ALL accepted papers in the research and applied data science tracks as well as the undergraduate consortium are required to prepare posters and have been assigned a poster spot. However, we understand that some authors may not be able to travel due to COVID regulations. If you are unable to travel and have made a “No Proxy” declaration (i.e., you could not find a registered proxy to present your paper), we will not be assigning a poster spot for your paper.

Poster Sesseion Schedule: We will have two poster presentation sessions:

6:30 pm to 8:30 pm on August 15 for papers from the following categories: Applied Data Science Track, and Undergraduate Consortium.

7:00 pm to 9:00 pm on August 16 for papers from the following categories: Research Track.

Both sessions will be held in the EXHIBIT HALL at the Washington DC Convention Center. For each poster session day, authors (or their registered proxies) should set up their posters between 3 pm to 5 pm. After the poster session ends, presenters should remove their posters. Any posters left in the Exhibit Hall may be discarded. Your paper is assigned a board number, which is used to locate the position of your poster. The board number can be found in the program booklet (to be distributed at the registration desk and to be made available on the KDD website).

Poster Presentation Instructions: The size limit for each poster is 1 meter by 1 meter (39.3 inches by 39.3 inches). Your posters need to fit within this size. Specific areas will be labeled in the Exhibit Hall to allow you to locate where to place your posters.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs):

Q: When is the deadline for preparing papers?
A: There is no deadline. Just make sure to present your poster during the corresponding session of your poster.
Q: What is the expected size of my poster?
A: The size limit for each poster is 1 meter by 1 meter (39.3 inches by 39.3 inches). The main constraint of your posters is that they should fit within that size. In other words, there are no constraints in terms of the orientation and size of the poster as long as it fits within the assigned area on the poster board.
Q: Can we pin our poster to the board?>
A: Yes. KDD will provide you with pushpins to mount the poster.
Q: How can we locate our poster board?
A: We will number each board and assign a board number to every paper. You can find the information on the program booklet/KDD website.
Q: Will KDD provide a sample poster template (.ppt file) to use?
A: No. We will not provide any template. You are free to use your own.
If you have any further questions or concerns, please contact poster chair at posters2022@kdd.org
